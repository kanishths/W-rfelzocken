Wurfelzocken

A Game of Stratergy and Luck.

A local two player game in which each player will get chance to roll a dice. The number on the dice will be added to the current score. The player will have an option to roll the dice again or to hold on to the current score. Rolling the dice again will come with a risk (i.e. if on rolling the dice one appears, the current score of the active player will reset to 0 and the game will pass on to the other player.) This will continue until one of the player scores a century. (100)

Enjoy The Game
